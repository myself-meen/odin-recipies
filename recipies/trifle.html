Trifle


Description

Trifle is a British dessert known for its beautiful layers and sweet surprise in every spoonful. Traditionally made with sponge cake, custard, fruit, jelly, and whipped cream, it’s both eye-catching and delicious. Just be sure not to follow Rachel Green’s accidental recipe—because no one wants beef sautéed with peas and onions in their dessert (except maybe Joey).

Steps

  1. Prepare cake layer: Cut sponge cake or pound cake into cubes.
  2. Layer jelly: Add fruit-flavored jelly over cake cubes in a large glass bowl. Chill until set.
  3. Add fruits: Layer with chopped fruits like strawberries, bananas, or mangoes.
  4. Add custard: Pour a thick layer of vanilla custard over the fruit.
  5. Top with whipped cream: Spread a generous amount of whipped cream on top.
  6. Decorate: Garnish with more fruit or sprinkles. Chill and serve cold
