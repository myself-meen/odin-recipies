Pizza

Description

Pizza is the ultimate crowd-pleaser, and no one loved it more than Joey Tribbiani. This iconic dish starts with a crisp yet chewy dough base, topped with tangy tomato sauce, melted cheese, and endless topping possibilities—from classic pepperoni to fresh veggies. Whether you're grabbing a New York-style slice or sharing a homemade pie, pizza always brings a slice of happiness.

Steps

  1. Make the dough (or use store-bought): Mix flour, yeast, water, and salt to form dough. Let it rise for 1–2 hours.
  2. Prepare toppings: Slice fresh mozzarella and tomatoes. Tear some fresh basil leaves.
  3. Roll the dough: Flatten the dough into a circle on a floured surface.
  4. Add sauce and toppings: Spread tomato sauce, add mozzarella slices, and tomatoes.
  5. Bake: Place in a preheated oven (220°C or 425°F) for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese bubbles.
  6. Garnish and serve: Add basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil. Slice and enjoy!
